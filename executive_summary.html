Context & Aim

OMSB has successfully piloted key digital initiatives (e-portfolio/MASAR, LMS, exam engine, AR/VR trials), but current operations rely on fragmented systems and siloed data. This 2026–2035 strategy establishes a phased roadmap to modernize operations, align with Oman Vision 2040, and position OMSB as a regional leader in digitally enabled medical education and regulation.

Strategic Ambition (Target State by 2035)

A cloud-secure, data-driven, user-centric ecosystem unifying residency/fellowship management, e-assessment, accreditation, credentialing, and CPD tracking — delivered via intuitive omni-channel services with trusted digital credentials.

Headline Outcomes by 2035

Process Excellence

60% faster certification/credentialing. 95% on-time exam results. Automated CPD compliance.

Interoperability

API-first integration across OMSB and two-way exchange with national/regional partners.

Analytics & AI

Predictive workforce modeling and early-alerting for at-risk trainees. AI-assisted learning/assessment.

User Experience

≥90% satisfaction through mobile-first, bilingual (AR/EN) services and credential wallets.

Governance & Security

ISO 27001-aligned ISMS. Full compliance with national ICT policies including accessibility.

Sustainability

Evergreen quarterly releases. ≥50% capacity headroom. Pathway to a self-financing MASAR model.

Self-Financing & Cost Management:

The strategy embeds sustainability beyond 2035. OMSB will target ≥20% cost-per-user efficiency by 2035 by optimizing OpEx (autoscaling, legacy contract retirement) and exploring self-financing models (white-labeling MASAR, joint ventures).

Six Strategic Pillars

  1. 1

    Modernize & automate core processes

    Residency, exams, CPD, accreditation, and certification processes to achieve 100% digital maturity.

  2. 2

    Unify data & break silos

    Establish strong governance and a national-grade integration layer with a Master Professional Index (MPI).

  3. 3

    Embed analytics & AI

    Integrate intelligence into decisions and operations, including prescriptive analytics for workforce planning.

  4. 4

    Deliver user-centric experiences

    Unified portal with SSO, mobile-first, bilingual, and accessible omni-channel services.

  5. 5

    Strengthen governance, security & compliance

    Align to ISO 27001 and establish IT/Data/Cyber sub-committees for continuous assurance.

  6. 6

    Empower workforce & foster innovation

    Build capabilities through a change-champions network, train-the-trainer, and continuous piloting.
