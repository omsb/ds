Project Rationale: The Why

The Oman Medical Specialty Board (OMSB) stands at a critical juncture in its digital evolution. Core processes and data remain fragmented across siloed systems, despite successful pilots of initiatives like the MASAR e-portfolio and AR/VR trials.

Strategy Mandate: Modernize operations, achieve alignment with the national digital agenda, and secure regional leadership in medical education by 2035.

The strategy directly supports Oman Vision 2040’s emphasis on excellent governance and human capital development.

Target State: The What

By 2035, OMSB will operate as a seamless, data-driven, and user-centric ecosystem unifying all core medical education functions (e.g., e-assessment, credentialing, CPD) under a single secure cloud platform.

Transformation Outcomes:

  • ✓Real-Time Engagement: Stakeholders receive personalized insights and trusted credentials.
  • ✓Operational Impact: Enables faster, paperless services and improved data-driven decision-making.

Strategic Vision

OMSB 2035: Seamless, Data-Driven Authority

“By 2035, OMSB will be the leading digitally integrated authority for postgraduate medical training, delivering seamless, data-driven, secure, and user-centric services that elevate educational quality and workforce readiness for Oman’s healthcare system.”

— Aligned with Oman Vision 2040.

Six Pillars of Transformation

These six foundational pillars structure the transformation, ensuring a comprehensive, systematic approach.

Modernize & Automate Core Processes

End-to-end digitization of OMSB services, from residency application to certification.

Unify Data & Break Down Silos

Single source of truth via a cloud data lake and Master Professional Index.

Embed Analytics & AI

Real-time dashboards, predictive models, early-alert systems for trainees.

Deliver User-Centric Experiences

Mobile-first, multi-channel services with a unified portal and SSO.

Strengthen Governance & Security

Cybersecurity frameworks, ISO 27001, full national policy compliance.

Empower Workforce & Foster Innovation

Change management, upskilling, and an “evergreen” IT environment.
