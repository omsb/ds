Budget & Financial Plan

(Investment & Phasing - A Funded Roadmap)

The OMSB Digital Transformation requires a **10-year investment envelope of OMR 22.216 million**, phased to align with the Implementation (2026–2028), Optimization & Expansion (2029–2032), and Continuous Innovation (2033–2036) phases outlined in the new strategy.

Total Investment Overview

Total Horizon

120 months (10 years)

Total Budget

OMR 22,216,400

Structure

  • Implementation Phase (30 months, 2026–2028) - **OMR 3,326,400**
  • Operational Phase (90 months, 2029–2036) - **OMR 18,890,000**

Implementation Phase (30 months - 2026–2028)

**Investment - OMR 3,326,400**
**Focus** - Establishing the MASAR platform, unifying data, and automating core workflows.

Months Activities Budget (OMR)
1–3 Program initiation, stakeholder engagement, Business Requirements Document (BRD) 800,000
4–9 Architecture setup, integration framework, core prototyping 1,300,000
10–24 Full-stack module development, testing, iterative refinement 926,400
25–30 Training, legacy data migration, platform go-live, stabilization 300,000

Operational Phase (90 months - 2029–2036)

**Investment - OMR 18,890,000**
**Focus** - Continuous support, cloud hosting, AI expansion, compliance, predictive analytics, and feature evolution.

Component Description Cost (OMR) Strategic Pillar(s) Supported
Perpetual Platform License One-time IP ownership for OMSB 5,000,000 Scale Sustainability
Annual Maintenance Patches, upgrades, SLA assurance (1M $\times$ 7.5 yrs) 7,500,000 Governance & Security, Sustainability
Support Team 12-person technical & admin team (90 months) 5,400,000 Governance & Security, Sustainability
Hosting Infrastructure Cloud hosting, backup, monitoring 990,000 Sustainability, User-Centric Experiences
Total Operational Investment: 18,890,000

Funding Sources

  • **Primary** - Government allocation (MOH & Ministry of Finance).
  • **Supplementary** - GCC & WHO grants, AI/innovation funds.
  • **Revenue-linked** - Digital credential verification, premium CPD services.
  • **Partnerships** - Public–private models for AR/VR, tele-simulation, AI modules.

ROI & Value Realization

  • **Efficiency Gains** - $>40\%$ manual workload reduction, 250+ processes digitized.
  • **Operational Savings** - Vendor consolidation saves $\sim15\%$ annually.
  • **Revenue Streams** - Digital verification, premium CPD, paid services.
  • **Strategic Impact** - OMSB positioned as regional leader in medical education & regulation.
